I'm building a Tripoley game with the following specifications:
Game Rules (from CardGameHeaven):
2-9 players (currently implementing 4 players: 1 human, 3 AI)
Uses standard 52-card deck
Features a special pot wheel with sections for:
Ace of Hearts
King of Hearts
Queen of Hearts
Jack of Hearts
Ten of Hearts
King-Queen of Hearts
8-9-10 (any suit)
Kitty
Pot
Project Structure:

src/
├── components/
│   ├── GameUI.js        (handles game visualization)
│   ├── PlayerHUD.js     (player's hand display)
│   └── GameScreen.js    (main game screen)
├── utils/
│   └── deck.js          (deck handling and dealing)
├── modes/               (game phases - not implemented yet)
│   ├── Michigan.js      (Michigan Rummy phase)
│   ├── Hearts.js        (Hearts phase)
│   └── Poker.js         (Poker phase)
├── Game.js              (game logic)
└── Player.js            (player logic)
